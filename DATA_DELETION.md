# Data Deletion Request — Hifz Musaa'id

**Last Updated:** May 24, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your personal data from Hifz Musaa'id ("the App"), and what we are able to delete on request. This page is referenced from the App's [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY) (Section 6) and from the Google Play Data Safety form.

## What personal data we hold about you

The App is designed to hold the minimum data necessary to operate. Depending on how you've used the App, the following items may exist on our backend tied to your device or account:

- **Device identifier.** A random UUID generated by the App on first launch. Tied to the App on your device only — not to your real-world identity.
- **Activation code redemption record** (if you redeemed a code). Includes the code string and the device UUID that redeemed it.
- **In-app-purchase receipt record** (if you subscribed via Google Play Billing or Apple StoreKit). Includes the validated purchase token, the resolved tier, and the device UUID.
- **Sign in with Apple record** (if you signed in). Includes the opaque Apple-issued user identifier, your email address (which may be an Apple-relay email), and optionally your name — only if you chose to share these via Apple's sign-in sheet.

We do **not** hold a name, phone number, location history, payment card information, contacts, calendar, photos, messages, browsing history, or any health or fitness data. See the [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY) for the complete list of what we collect and do not collect.

## How to request deletion

To request deletion of your personal data, email us at **shabbir.com@gmail.com** with the subject line **"Data Deletion Request"**. Include in the body of the email:

1. **The device UUID** associated with your account (optional but speeds up our response). You can find this in the App at **Settings → About → Diagnostic information**. If you don't have access to the device anymore, that's fine — see step 2.
2. **The email address you used to sign in with Apple** (if you signed in). If you used Apple's email-relay feature, this will be a `@privaterelay.appleid.com` address.
3. **The activation code you redeemed** (if you redeemed one). The 12-character alphanumeric string.

You don't need to provide all three — any one of them lets us locate and delete your data.

## What we delete

On receipt of a valid deletion request, we will delete:

- Your device identifier and any associated activation code redemption record on our backend.
- Your Sign in with Apple record, if any.
- Your in-app-purchase receipt record on our backend. (Note: this does **not** cancel an active Apple or Google subscription — those are managed by Apple or Google respectively. See the next section.)
- Any rate-limit log lines associated with your device UUID (these typically auto-expire after 5 minutes anyway).

We will respond within **30 days** to confirm that the deletion has taken place. If we need additional information to verify your identity before deleting, we will respond within 7 days requesting it.

## What we cannot delete

The following items are not under our control and must be handled directly with the relevant party:

- **Active subscriptions on Google Play.** To cancel an Android subscription, open the Google Play Store app → tap your profile icon → **Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Hifz Musaa'id → Cancel subscription**. Or visit <https://play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions>.
- **Active subscriptions on the Apple App Store.** To cancel an iOS subscription, open the iOS **Settings** app → tap your Apple ID at the top → **Subscriptions → Hifz Musaa'id → Cancel Subscription**. Or visit <https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions>.
- **App data stored locally on your device.** This includes downloaded audio files (encrypted on disk), saved program configurations, and streak data. To delete all local data, uninstall the App from your device.
- **Records we are legally required to retain.** For example, transaction records required by tax law. These are retained only as long as the law requires and are deleted thereafter.

## Identity verification

To prevent abuse, we may verify your identity before processing a deletion request. Typically this involves sending a one-time verification code to:

- The email address on your Sign in with Apple record (if you signed in), **or**
- The email address you submitted the deletion request from, if it matches our records.

If we cannot verify your identity, we will still delete data tied to the device UUID or activation code you provided — but we will not delete any Sign in with Apple record unless we can confirm the request came from the account owner.

## Questions

For any question about this process, or to follow up on a deletion request you've already submitted, email **shabbir.com@gmail.com**. We respond within 30 days as required by GDPR / CCPA / general privacy law.
